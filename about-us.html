The Destination

Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh

Cox's Bazar is a coastal town in Chittagong Division, on Bangladesh's Bay of Bengal coast — home to the longest natural sea beach in Asia, the country's only coral island at Saint Martin, and a Rakhine cultural history spanning over a thousand years. This site brings together the beach, the wider destinations, food, culture, and practical travel information in one place.

Official Tourism Resources

Bangladesh Tourism Board & Parjatan Corporation

For official national travel information — including the Sundarbans, Dhaka, and Bangladesh's hill districts — visit the Bangladesh Tourism Board's portal, Beautiful Bangladesh. The Bangladesh Parjatan Corporation, the country's national tourism organisation, also operates hotels and tourist facilities directly in Cox's Bazar.
