Suggested Itineraries

How long do you need?

Weekend Escape

2–3 Days

1

Arrive, settle into Kolatoli or Sugandha, sunset at Sugandha Beach.

2

Morning at Laboni (horse riding, kite flying), afternoon along Marine Drive to Himchari waterfall and Inani Beach, seafood dinner in Kolatoli.

3

If extending: Burmese Market morning, depart afternoon.

The Classic Week

5 Days

1

Arrive, Laboni Beach, sunset at Sugandha.

2

Full Marine Drive day — Himchari, Inani.

3

Culture day — Rakhain Village, Aggameda Khyang, Burmese Market, Shutki Palli.

4

Moheshkhali Island — Adinath Temple, salt flats, speedboat crossing.

5

Activities (surfing, parasailing) and departure.

Island Explorer

7 Days · Nov 1 – Jan 31 only

1–2

As the Classic Week — Laboni, Sugandha, Marine Drive.

3

Day trip to Saint Martin's (book ahead — daily cap applies).

4

Culture day.

5

Himchari + Inani.

6

Moheshkhali.

7

Free day / departure.

Saint Martin's is closed to tourism Feb–Oct — this itinerary only works in peak season.

Beyond the Coast

Make It Part of a Bigger Trip

Cox's Bazar pairs beautifully with a wider Bangladesh journey. Four destinations extend naturally from time on the coast.

🏙️

Dhaka

The capital rewards 2–3 days. Old Dhaka's Ahsan Manzil, Lalbagh Fort, and the Sadarghat river port are unmissable. Connected to Cox's Bazar by a 1-hour flight or overnight bus.

🌿

The Sundarbans

The world's largest mangrove delta — UNESCO-listed, Bengal tiger territory. Accessible via Khulna or Mongla on 2–3 day boat tours. Best reached via Dhaka.

🍃

Sylhet

Rolling tea gardens, the Hazrat Shah Jalal shrine, and the eerie Ratargul swamp forest. In Bangladesh's northeast — best reached via Dhaka.

⛰️

Chittagong Hill Tracts

Forested hills, Chakma and Marma communities, Kaptai Lake, and waterfalls. Bandarban is just 3 hours from Cox's Bazar — the most natural add-on to this trip. Some areas require permits.

For full national itinerary planning, visit the Bangladesh Tourism Board's official portal — Beautiful Bangladesh ↗
