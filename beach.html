Cox's Bazar

The Beach

120 unbroken kilometres from Laboni to Teknaf — one of the longest uninterrupted stretches of sand anywhere on Earth.

The Zones

One beach. Many faces.

Main Beach

Laboni & Kolatoli

The lively heart of Cox's Bazar — kites, horses, food stalls, and the longest view of open sea you'll ever stand in front of.

📍 Town centre 🕐 All day
Coral Coast

Inani Beach

32 km south — coral stones embedded in the sand, teal water, dramatically quieter. The beach that photographers come for.

📍 32 km south 🕐 Morning best
Sunset Point

Sugandha Beach

The preferred sunset-watching stretch — the beach faces directly west, and when the sky turns copper, there is nowhere better to stand.

📍 Central 🕐 Dusk

The Road

Marine Drive — 80 km along the edge of the world

Built along the full length of the beach, Marine Drive is one of South Asia's most scenic coastal roads. On one side, the Bay of Bengal. On the other, forested hills. Take it by CNG, bike, or on foot — there is no wrong way.

Light & Time

When to be on the beach

Dawn

5:30–6:30 AM — the beach belongs to fishermen and early walkers. Absolute silence. The trawlers leaving harbour before first light is one of the most memorable sights in Cox's Bazar.

Golden Hour

5:30–6:15 PM — the beach faces west. On a clear evening, the sky turns copper, the wet sand mirrors the colours, and the horizon is unobstructed for 120 kilometres. This is the reason people come.

Midday

10 AM–3 PM — full energy. Kites, horses, food vendors, beach cricket. The fishing harbour at Nazirartek is most active mid-morning when the boats return. Worth timing a visit.

Know Before You Go

Beach essentials

🌊

Swimming

The Bay of Bengal has strong currents. Swim near the flagged zones at Laboni and Kolatoli. Avoid swimming alone at low tide.

☀️

Best Season

November to February for calm seas and clear skies. June–September is monsoon — rough seas, but dramatically beautiful green hills.

🚌

Getting Around

CNG auto-rickshaws run the full Marine Drive. Negotiate before you ride. Beach bikes and horse rides are available at Laboni.

See the islands too

Saint Martin's coral island is 9 km offshore — reachable by ship from Cox's Bazar during the Nov–Jan season.
